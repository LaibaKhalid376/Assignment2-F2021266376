Rashid Khan

Rashid Khan is a talented Afghan cricketer known for his outstanding performance in the international arena, particularly in T20 cricket. He has earned the title of "world's best T20 bowler" and has been named captain of the Afghanistan national team in all formats of the game.


Imran Khan

Imran Khan is a Pakistani politician, former cricketer, and the current Prime Minister of Pakistan. He led the national cricket team to victory in the 1992 Cricket World Cup and founded the Pakistan Tehreek-e-Insaf (PTI) political party in 1996. As Prime Minister, he has focused on improving the economy, reducing corruption, and increasing education and healthcare access for all citizens. His leadership and policies continue to shape Pakistan's political landscape.


Sikandar Raza

Sikandar Raza is a professional cricketer from Zimbabwe who is known for his all-round abilities. He has represented his country in all formats of the game and has been an important player for Zimbabwe. Raza is known for his aggressive batting style and his off-spin bowling, which has helped him pick up crucial wickets in matches.


Shaheen Afridi

Shaheen Afridi is a young and talented Pakistani cricketer known for his exceptional bowling skills. He made his debut in 2018 and has quickly established himself as a key player in the team. With his impressive pace and accuracy, Shaheen has become a consistent wicket-taker in both the shorter and longer formats of the game. He is seen as a bright prospect for the future of Pakistan cricket and has already made a name for himself on the international stage.
